United States of America

Known for its motto "In God We Trust," the USA is a land of diverse landscapes, cultures, and iconic landmarks.

By: Ugwor Chiedozie Nwaeligwe ()

  • 1. Statue of Liberty

    The Statue of Liberty, located on Liberty Island in New York Harbor, is a universal symbol of freedom and democracy. A gift from France, this colossal statue has welcomed immigrants arriving in New York for over a century.

    Learn more

  • 2. Golden Gate Bridge

    The Golden Gate Bridge, spanning the Golden Gate Strait in San Francisco, California, is a remarkable feat of engineering and one of the most photographed bridges in the world.

    Learn more

  • 3. Grand Canyon

    The Grand Canyon, a UNESCO World Heritage site in Arizona, offers breathtaking views of layered red rock that reveal millions of years of geological history. It's a must-see natural wonder for any traveler.

    Learn more

  • 4. Mount Rushmore

    Mount Rushmore National Memorial in South Dakota features the carved faces of U.S. presidents George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, Theodore Roosevelt, and Abraham Lincoln, symbolizing American history and ideals.
